Project: Flight Price Tracker



Goal:

Users enter flight details and get email alerts when prices change.



Input:

\- From

\- To

\- Travel date

\- Email

\- Optional: target price



Output:

\- Email alerts:

&#x20; - Price drop

&#x20; - Lowest price

&#x20; - Price increase

&#x20; - 1-week reminder



Tech Stack:

\- Frontend: React (host on GitHub Pages)

\- Backend: FastAPI (Python)

\- Database: SQLite

\- Flight API: Amadeus

\- Email: Gmail SMTP

\- Hosting: Render



Keep everything simple and minimal.
